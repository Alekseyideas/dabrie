PRESERVE YOUR MEDIA

CTM Andre Debrie offers a complete range of film equipment: inspection and rewinding tables, traditional and digital viewing table, cleaning machines, restoration equipment and scanners.

About us

Les Établissements André Debrie and Pathé are the main inventors and manufacturers of the French professional film equipment. Since 1898, DEBRIE has been constantly innovating and designing new solutions for film preservation. It has since registered more than 400 patents in France.

Today, Debrie offers a complete range of film equipment: inspection and rewinding tables, traditional and digital viewing tables, cleaning machines, restoration equipment and scanners.

Technical specifications

Film types and formats

  • Negative, Intermediate, Interpositive,
  • Print, color or B&W.
  • 8/S8 – 16/S16 – 35/S35 mm
  • 9.5 – 17.5 – 28 mm (option)

Light source and scanning system

  • Integrating sphere
  • Cool LED light source .
  • Real-time digitization in 25 p/s for 2K
  • Speed rewind: 4 times of real speed
  • Ballistic Following Scanning (BFS®) of the frame
  • Exposure time in front of the camera =0,004 seconde
  • Increasing the time of exposure in comparison with the flash systeme (Exposure time Flash system 400 times smaller)
  • Ultra low noise camera with Bayer pattern
  • High resolution lenses
  • Ratio: 4:3 – 16:9 – 1.85
  • Self-adjusting motorized frame size and focus 2, 3, 4 perforation in 35mm

    • Sound readers (see options)

    • Optical sound reader COMMOPT, all format types
    • Magnetic sound reader COMMAG or SEPMAG, all format types
    • Synchronization with a double-tape reader by bi-phase signal

Output and format

  • Progressive camera with 5K sensor (5120x3840)
  • Resolution 4K (4096x3112), 2K (2048x1556) or HD (1920x1080) 5K, 4K, 2K, HD-SDI 25p ou 50i, SD-SDI 4/3 – 16/9
  • DPX couleur/N&B 10/16 bit (ANSI / SMPTE 268M-2003)
  • Uncompressed AVI
  • Proxy H264
  • WAV/BMF
  • Bi-phase output, synchronization Genlock input and basic RS422 protocol
  • Audio output (2 stereo channel) — SDI embedded (BNC) — AES UER (BNC) — Analog (XLR)

Film transport

  • Film capacity: 2,000 feet (600 m)
  • Capstan drive system
  • Continuous motion servo film drive for constant film tension
  • Laser detection
  • Shrunken film handling up to 5%
  • Cleaning by Particle Transfer Rollers — PTR (option)

Workstation

  • The most powerful workstation HP Z series (840) of the last generation
  • High performance preconfigured with Microsoft Windows7 — 64 bit — professional
  • 16/9 LCD monitor
  • PCIe slots for high speed connectivity (Giga Ethernet — fiber channel)
  • RAID0 internal storage 12 TB

Dimensions

  • Width: 105 cm
  • Depth: 71 cm
  • Height: 182 cm
  • Cratin size: 150x96x230 cm
  • Net weight: 300 kg
  • Gross weight: 460 kg

Operating environment

  • 15°C to 30°C
